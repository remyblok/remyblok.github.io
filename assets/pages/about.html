Hello there!

I'm Remy Blok. J is from my middle name, forming a nice RJB. Not to be confused with RGB...I know it's all the hype, I stick with purple.

They say I'm a Software Architect, but honestly I'm more a software tinkerer. I love just playing around and new stuff and finding boundaries and trying to go beyond. While doing that I actually do try to make workable software. I find solutions for problems you never knew you had, or maybe you did and that's why you ended up here at my blog.

At school I learned Java, but I quickly switched to .Net. From there I learned quickly enough! C#, WinForms, Asp.net, WPF, WCF, Asp.Net MVC, and all the standards and technologies that come with those stacks. Gradually I shifted more to integration and back-end systems. First with BizTalk, later with all the goodies Azure has to offer.

Nowadays I'm again shifting focus more to the Power Platform. It is becoming more important in the Microsoft stack every day. Everything is moving pretty fast on the Power Platform, with many new features rolling out continuously. So many opportunities for me to tinker around. My goal is to share some of the good stuff with you!
